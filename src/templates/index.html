Heart Disease

Cardiovascular disease (CVD) is a class of diseases that involve the heart or blood vessels. Cardiovascular diseases are the leading cause of death worldwide except Africa. Together CVD resulted in 17.9 million deaths (32.1%) in 2015, up from 12.3 million (25.8%) in 1990. You can use our predictive model to predict whether you have CVD or not.

Kidney Disease

Kidney disease, or renal disease, technically referred to as nephropathy, is damage to or disease of a kidney, Nephritis an inflammatory kidney disease and has several types according to the location of the inflammation. Inflammation can be diagnosed by blood test. Nephrosis is a non-inflammatory kidney disease. Make sure you try our predictive model in order to see your result.

Breast cancer

Breast cancer most commonly presents as a lump that feels different from the rest of the breast tissue. More than 80% of cases are discovered when a person detects such a lump with the fingertips. Verify whether you have Malignant or Benign. Our Machine Learning model will try it’s best to give the correct result.

Brain tumor

A brain tumor occurs when abnormal cells form within the brain There are two main types of tumors: malignant tumors and benign (non-cancerous) tumors. These can be further classified as primary tumors which start within the brain, and secondary tumors, which most commonly have spread from tumors located outside the brain, known as brain metastasis tumors.

Tools & Technologies

We are using machine learning algorithms like Logistic Regression, Decision Tree Classifier, Random Forests, as well as Artificial Neural Networks in order to train our model along with the ML libraries such as scikit-learn, numpy, pandas, matplotlib, seaborn & Tensorflow.

MachineLearning

For image processing, we are using convolutional neural networks (CNN) along with the Inception model to predict the best result. We are training over about 5k-10k image dataset with model accuracy more than 85% each. We are using scikit-learn, Tensorflow & Keras for models.

DeepLearning
